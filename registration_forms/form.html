Welcome to ITP Tips


Registering as an ITP expert gives you the opportunity to help out your classmates in a unique way. Once you sign up as an expert in a specific field, you will be added to a list of potential experts who may be called upon by students seeking quick counsel.

If you receive a call, you will have up to two minutes to answer questions and direct the caller to other resources that may help address their problem.

Using the below form, please indicate the number where you would like to be called as well as the dates and time window when are available to receive calls.
