ITP Tips is a Redial project that Once you register as an expert in one of the fields below, you will be added to a list of potential experts who may be called upon by students seeking quick counsel.

If you receive a call, you will have up to two minutes to answer questions and direct the caller to other resources that may help address their problem. After two minutes, the call will automatically disconnect.

Using the below form, please indicate the phone number where you would like to be called as well as the dates and time window when are available to receive calls.

  • This means you will be available during this window and willing to answer calls. If you have class or you are busy, simply don't pick up and the caller will be directed to another expert.
